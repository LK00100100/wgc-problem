Wolf, Goat, Cabbage Problem

A classic conundrum.
You have a wolf, a goat, one big cabbage, and boat. You are trying to get to the other side of the river with everything. However, there is a problem. You cannot leave certain elements alone.

  • The wolf will eat the goat.
  • The goat will eat the cabbage.

Click an item to take it on and off the boat. Click the boatman to move the boat. Once the boat moves away, something may eat something.
